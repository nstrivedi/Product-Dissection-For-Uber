**Project Overview :**

A full product dissection of Uber, One of the high demand bike, auto, traditional taxi and private-hire
transportation, along with a 7-entity relational database schema design.

**Skills Demonstrated :**

SQL · ER Diagram Design · Product Thinking · Real-World Problem Analysis

**Key Highlights :**

Identified 4 core real-world problems Uber solves
Designed 7 entities: Users, Drivers, Vehicles, Rides, Payments, Ratings, Promotion & Ride Promotions
Rides is the central hub — 6 out of 7 relationships pass through it
